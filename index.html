My DevOps Journey at JAAJ

Fall 2024 Co-op Experience

Abstract/Introduction

During my Fall 2024 co-op work term at JAAJ, I worked as a DevOps Developer. This experience offered hands-on exposure to Azure DevOps, focusing on automating ticket management processes. Through this website, I aim to share insights into the work I did, the skills I developed, and how this term contributed to my professional growth in the field of DevOps.

Information about the Employer

JAAJ is a technology company specializing in DevOps and software development solutions. They focus on optimizing cloud-based environments, streamlining the software development lifecycle (SDLC), and solving client challenges through cutting-edge technology. During my term, I had the opportunity to work on DevOps automation projects that played a crucial role in enhancing efficiency and accuracy in task management.

Goals

  • Learn Azure DevOps: I gained confidence navigating tools and automating workflows.
  • Improve Problem-Solving Skills: I approached challenges thoughtfully and found practical solutions.
  • Practice Python Scripting: I improved my scripting skills for automation tasks.
  • Collaborate in a Team Environment: I built teamwork skills by contributing in daily meetings.
  • Work Independently: I delivered reliable results with minimal supervision.

Job Description

My role at JAAJ focused on automating ticket management within Azure DevOps. I worked on scripting solutions to ensure tasks were updated systematically based on project progress, deadlines, and hierarchical relationships.

Conclusions

My co-op term at JAAJ allowed me to bridge academic learning with practical experience in the field of DevOps. By automating ticket workflows in Azure DevOps, I developed skills in scripting, problem-solving, and collaboration, which are essential for a career in technology. This experience has prepared me for future roles by giving me confidence in tackling real-world challenges.
